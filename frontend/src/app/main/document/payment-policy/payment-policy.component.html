CANCELLATION AND REFUND

Petcoset can help you find replacement Service Providers when Service Providers cancel Bookings near the start date of the service period identified in the Booking. The below cancellation policies will not be applicable if any booking gets cancelled due to “Force Majeure” (earthquake, cyclone, pandemics etc.) or impractical/ impossible to serve the booking.

  • Cancelled by Service provider: If a Service Provider cancels a Booking prior to or during the service period identified in the Booking, Company will refund the fees paid by the Pet Owner for Pet Care Services not provided, as well as any service charge paid to Petcoset.com. Full refund will be initiated and completed within 7days of receiving Legitimate refund. If you are a service provider and cancel accepted Bookings without justification for more than 3 times, Petcoset may block your account till further notice and you can file “re appeal” request to our Grievance committee.

  • Cancellations by Pet Owner: If a Pet Owner cancels a Booking prior to or during the service period specified in a Booking, we will refund fees in accordance with the cancellation policy selected by the Service Provider on the Petcoset Service. If we determine in our reasonable discretion that a Service Provider has failed to provide Pet Care Services as agreed with the Pet Owner or is otherwise in breach of these Terms, then we may, in our reasonable discretion, cancel a Booking and/or issue a full or partial refund to a Pet Owner.

In all cases Refund and cancelation request by both the parties shall intimated through care@petcost.com
